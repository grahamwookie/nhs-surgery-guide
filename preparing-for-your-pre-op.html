Your pre-op appointment

During your pre-op the nurse will ask you about your health and do things like:

  • take your blood pressure
  • weigh you
  • test you for bacteria, such as MRSA

You may need other tests, like blood tests and you may also speak to the anaesthetist.

If you take medicine, bring it with you (including vitamins and herbal supplements).

The nurse will tell you when you have to arrive at the hospital.
